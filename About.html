SWAR YOG PEETH

We are a yoga school located in the spiritual town of Rishikesh, India. Our experienced teachers aim to empower individuals through authentic yoga education. We believe in promoting holistic living and offer a supportive environment for students of all levels. Join us on a journey to transform your mind, body, and soul.

Mission
To empower individuals from all walks of life to transform their mind, body and soul through authentic and accessible yoga education in Rishikesh
Vision
Our vision is to create a community where people from all backgrounds come together to experience the physical, mental and spiritual benefits of yoga and promote holistic living.
Values
We believe in fostering a culture of compassion, respect, inclusiveness, sustainability, personal growth and development, and are committed to creating a welcoming and supportive learning environment for all.
We’ve been fostering holistic wellness through exceptional Yoga Teacher Training, Meditation Courses, and Ayurveda Courses for past 10 years.
Swar Yog Peeth is a registered meditation and spiritual school located in the heart of Rishikesh, India. Verified by Uttarakhand Tourism (GoI) as a Kendra for yoga and meditation, the institute is also registered as a Private Limited Company under the Government of India Act 2013. Our mission is to provide authentic and accessible spiritual experiences, with a focus on meditation and other wellness activities, yoga courses, Ayurveda treatments, spiritual tours, and more.
At Swar Yog Peeth, we believe in the traditional and ancient teachings of the Vedas and Puranas, and strive to offer a supportive and inclusive environment for people from all backgrounds. Established in 2014, we have become a popular destination for spiritual seekers from around the world, and are dedicated to fostering personal growth and development through our offerings.
In addition to our wide range of meditation and yoga courses, Swar Yog Peeth also offers Ayurveda teacher training and consultations, as well as a range of Ayurvedic treatments and programs. Whether you’re looking to deepen your spiritual practice, learn about traditional Ayurvedic techniques, or simply take some time for self-care and rejuvenation, Swar Yog Peeth is here to support you on your journey.
