Course Overview Swar Yog Peeth, located in the spiritual city of Rishikesh, offers a comprehensive 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training program that is accredited by Yoga Alliance, USA. With a strong focus on Hatha, Ashtanga, and Vinyasa Flow yoga teachings, this program is designed to help you become a fully-certified yoga teacher, equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to begin your career as a Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT) anywhere in the world.
At Swar Yog Peeth, we believe that yoga is much more than just physical postures. That’s why our program is designed to explore the physical, philosophical, spiritual, and mental aspects of yoga. Our curriculum includes in-depth studies of Bandha, Mudra, Kriya, Sanskrit, Mantra, Ayurveda, Philosophy, Anatomy & Physiology, and much more. Our aim is to help you not just understand the practice of yoga, but also the secrets of a yogic lifestyle and how to live a healthy, balanced, and blissful life.
Our residential program of Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh is held at our peaceful yoga and meditation centre, surrounded by nature and providing a serene and harmonious environment for your learning and personal growth. Our small class sizes ensure that you receive personalized attention from our experienced and knowledgeable teachers, helping you to deepen your knowledge and skills in yoga therapy and your personal practice.
At Swar Yog Peeth, we are dedicated to supporting your journey towards becoming a confident and knowledgeable yoga instructor. Whether you are planning to teach yoga professionally or simply looking for self-development, our 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training program offers a once-in-a-lifetime experience that will help you to emerge as a fully-certified yoga teacher with strong and solid teaching skills.
