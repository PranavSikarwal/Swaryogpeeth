Overview Of Yoga Teacher Training Course In Rishikesh

Yoga Teacher Training (YTT) is a complete, practical program designed for anyone who wants to become a certified yoga instructor.
The 200–500 hours required for the yoga training program include the study of philosophy, anatomy, teaching methodology, and a variety of yoga modules



Here is an overview of yoga teacher training at Yin Yang Yoga Academy, Rishikesh:

Hatha Yoga:

Hatha yoga synchronizes breathing with physical postures for achieving whole-body and mind
balance.

Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga:

A form of dynamic, flowing yoga where attachment to breath and its synchrony with strength,
flexibility, and awareness in meditation are linked together within a designed pattern of poses.

Restorative yoga:

A very gentle practice that invites the practitioner toward relaxation, deep rest, stress
reduction, and general health improvement.

Pranayama:

The conscious flow of breath while doing yoga in order to develop better physical health along
with sharpness of mind.

Philosophy of Yoga

The inquiry into the ancient principles that dominate this practice and spiritual insight,
emphasizing that mind, body, and spirit should be unionized so as to assure an individual's well-being.

Mudra and Bandhas:

the usage of hand gestures and energy locks to help with channeling pranic energies into
overall better mental and physical health.

Meditation:

A process by which practicing mindfulness brings a clear mind, peace within, and emotional
strength developing through focussing or reflective attention. Yoga Alignment Adjustment: yoga
alignment adjustment means modifying body positions performed with direct guidance and
intention for proper effect and safe achievement as regards the overall effectiveness relating
to a particular yogic postural position.

The Anatomy and Physiology of Yoga:

The Anatomy and Physiology of Yoga introduces the students to the scientific approach to the
perception of the structure of the body as well as its function, equipping them for the safe
performance of yoga knowledgeably.

Chanting mantra:

An outer repetition of the sacred sounds or even phrases that bring about mindfulness and, a
sense of inner peace followed by spiritual connection within the boundary of yoga practice.

Shatkarma:

The six yogic purifications intend to cleanse the body and mind, attuning them to health and
balance through certain practices of cleaning the nose and purifying the digestive tract.

Methodology in teaching:

Methodology in teaching yoga involves all the effective instructional techniques inclusive of
classroom management and communication skills with an aim of making the place of learning
supportive and enriching one for the learners.e.

What is Yoga Alliance, USA Certification

Acquiring certification from Yoga Alliance USA is a great honor to every yoga teacher and school to show conformity to the international acceptance standards. This certification is granted by the renowned international non-profit Yoga Alliance, stating that an individual or school meets specific requirements regarding yoga education and training. This certificate improves a certified teacher or the school's reputation, it denotes that the certified teacher has committed high standards of teaching, and ensures increased safety measures in classes etc. The students feel comfortable knowing that the certified teachers or the school have proven their ability and dedication to teaching good yoga.
